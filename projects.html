Teaching Robot

As part of my university degree, in my final year I completed a personal project in which I researched the use of robotics for education in computer science, as well as methods for controlling the robots movement. In order to test my research I built a diy robot that could be used within schools as a teaching resource. The robot was made of cheap, widely available components such to keep the costs low. The control systems were programmed in C on an arduino microcontroller.
